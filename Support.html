- Types of bullying include verbal bullying, physical bullying, social bullying, and cyber bullying. Stop Bullying

- Bullying can be stopped or prevented through standing up for one another, giving compliments, and being a friend to someone who has been bullied. Stomp Out Bullying

- It states that 1 in every 5 children report getting bullied. Also, 6% of males get physically bullied; however, 4% of females get physically bullied. Additionally, 18% of females get verbally bullied, but 9% of males get verbally bullied. Furthermore, 41% of students claim that they believe the bullying will reoccur. National Bullying Prevention Center
